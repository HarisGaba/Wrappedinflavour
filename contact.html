FAQ

Common Questions

How do I place an order? ▼

The easiest way to order is via WhatsApp! Just tap the "Order Now" button on any page, send us a message with your order, and we'll confirm everything and arrange delivery or pickup in Mississauga.

Do you offer delivery in Mississauga? ▼

Yes! We offer delivery within Mississauga, ON. Contact us via WhatsApp or our contact form to arrange your delivery. Pickup is also available!

Do you have vegan/gluten-free options? ▼

Absolutely! We offer several vegan and vegetarian wraps including The Green Goddess and The Falafel Feast. Gluten-free tortillas are available for an additional $1.50.

Can I place a bulk/group order? ▼

Yes! For group orders of 10 or more, please contact us via WhatsApp or email at least 24 hours in advance and we'll make sure everything is ready hot and fresh.

Do you accommodate allergies? ▼

We take allergies very seriously. Please inform us of any dietary restrictions when ordering and we'll do our best to accommodate. Our kitchen handles common allergens including wheat, dairy, nuts, and soy.

Are you available for events in Mississauga? ▼

Yes! We cater events throughout Mississauga and the Greater Toronto Area. Email us at catering@wrappedinflavour.ca or send us a message on WhatsApp to get a custom quote.
