Meet the Chef

The Heart Behind
Every Bite

Founder & Head Chef · Wrapped in Flavour

What started as a passion for feeding loved ones became something the whole community fell in love with. Growing up surrounded by rich culinary traditions from across the globe, I always believed that food is more than just a meal — it's a story, a memory, a warm embrace.

In 2021, I turned my home kitchen into the heart of Wrapped in Flavour right here in Mississauga, Ontario. Every wrap I make carries the same love as the very first one I ever made for my family. From my secret garlic toum to my house-marinated chicken — everything is made fresh, by hand, with no shortcuts.

Canada gave me a home, and through every flavourful bite, I give a little piece of my heart back to this beautiful community.
