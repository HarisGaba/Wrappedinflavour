How It Began

Born from Passion & Spice

Wrapped in Flavour started as a family dream — a deep love for bold, global flavours mixed with the warmth and multicultural spirit that makes Canada so special.

What began in a home kitchen in Mississauga, Ontario, feeding friends and family, quickly grew into something the whole community wanted to taste. We believe food is connection, and every wrap we make carries that spirit.

From the first bite of our house-marinated chicken to the final drizzle of our secret garlic sauce — we pour our heart into every single order. Mississauga gave us a community, and we give back with every flavourful bite.

What We Stand For

Our Mission

🌿

Fresh, Always

We prep everything fresh every single day. No freezers. No pre-packaged shortcuts. Just real, honest homemade food made with care.

🍁

Canadian Roots

We're proud to be Canadian. Based in Mississauga, ON, we source local ingredients wherever possible, supporting our community.

🌍

Global Inspiration

Canada is home to people from everywhere. We celebrate that diversity in every recipe — from shawarma to chimichurri to falafel.

💚

Eco-Conscious

We use compostable packaging, minimize food waste, and partner with local composting programs to reduce our footprint.

Our Journey

Milestones & Moments

🏠
2021

The Beginning

Started as a home-based business in Mississauga, delivering wraps to friends, family, and neighbours in the local community.

📱
2022

Going Social

Launched @wrappedinflavour on Instagram. Our authentic content and amazing food sparked a loyal following almost overnight.

🎪
2023

First Pop-Ups

Began attending local food markets and pop-up events in Mississauga. Sold out at every event. The community fell in love.

🚀
2024–2025

Growing Strong

Expanded our menu, built a professional online presence, and continue growing our community of flavour lovers across Mississauga.
